## Evidence required for each source

Record all of the following before changing a source from HOLD:

- Original filename and an owner-controlled copy of the original file
- Photographer/creator and copyright owner
- How and when the image was obtained
- Written license or assignment evidence, its storage reference, and date
- Permission for commercial website, social, Pinterest, and paid-ad use as applicable
- Permission to crop, add overlays, and make other derivatives
- Any duration, territory, attribution, or channel restrictions
- Property, person, and privacy-release review
- Final approver and approval date

## Release rule

A replacement may be released only after the original is delivered, the rightsholder is identified, written evidence covers the intended channel and derivative use, private details are removed, an approved replacement overlay from `PUBLISHING-HOLD.md` is used, and a human records final approval. If any element cannot be documented, replace the photo with new owner-created photography or a separately licensed image.
