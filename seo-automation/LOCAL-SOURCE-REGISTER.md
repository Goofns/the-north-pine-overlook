# Local guide source register

Last reviewed: July 15, 2026

This register supports the human fact-check gate for Bailey-area guide content. It is not permission to publish automatically. Before a guide is published or materially updated, open the linked manager or operator page and recheck access, closures, fees, reservations, fire restrictions, and regulations.

## Local stops and museums

Use each business's or museum's current operator page before mentioning hours, events, availability, or admission. The reviewed guide links to the Park County Historical Society for McGraw Memorial Park and to the current operator or tourism listing for the other named stops. Seasonal operation and business status must be rechecked; do not hard-code hours.

## Automation guardrails

- Treat this file as review evidence, not a live-data feed.
- Recheck every time-sensitive claim on the day of final human approval.
- Never infer property drive times from public route information.
- Link live alerts instead of embedding temporary fire restrictions or closures in evergreen copy.
- Record the reviewer and date in the content item's **Facts requiring verification** field.
- If an official source conflicts with another official source, omit the disputed number and link the live source.
